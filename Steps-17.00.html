4.       Map for Total Deaths by County.


Detailed Instructions:
·       From the Visualizations pane, select Map. 
·       Drag and drop County and State from the MD_COVID19_DEATHS table in Location.
·       Drag and drop Total Deaths measure in Bubble size.
·       Drag and drop Total Deaths YoY% and Death Rate measures in Tooltips.

5.       Add a legend to the map.


Detailed Instructions: 
·       In Power BI Desktop, go to the Insert ribbon and click on Image.
·       Select Map Legend from the Project Essentials folder.
·       Resize it to fit the map.
·       In the Home ribbon, click on Text box. Type LOW and HIGH in the box.
·       To format the text box, click on it and go to General in the Format pane.
·       In Effects, turn Background On; Color: #FFFFFF; Transparency: 100%.
·       Move the text box above the legend; select both and go to the Format ribbon.
·       Click on the Group button and select Group from the drop-down list. 
·       Move the legend to the bottom of the map.
