Insert canvas background 14


Detailed Instructions:
 
1.       Go to the Report view in Power BI Desktop. 
2.       In the Visualizations pane, click on Format page and select Canvas background from the Image menu.
3.        Choose Fill from the Image fit menu to fill the canvas with the image. 
4.       Turn the transparency to 85%.
