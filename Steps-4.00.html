Challenges Encountered 4
While this project has been an eye opener, I encountered a few challenges including:

1.      Finding a dataset. My search for a dataset that would give me the opportunity to explore a relevant public health topic was a challenging task. I had to go through 3 datasets before getting approved to work on the current one. However, I believe that my search was worth the time and effort.
2.      Organizing the dataset. Once the dataset was found, understanding the data and creating a dashboard that would correctly depict the reality of confirmed COVID-19 deaths in Maryland was a challenging task. The original dataset only provided the cumulative COVID deaths. It was necessary to enter the daily death numbers manually since there was no single pre-existing dataset that recorded daily death numbers for Maryland. This proved to be quite a challenging and time-consuming endeavor. 
3.      Key Performance Indicators. While industry KPIs are available, finding the ones that will better depict my specific dataset was a challenge. They have to make sense of what I want to display on my dashboard.
4.    Creating map visual. By far my biggest challenge was creating a map of Maryland as one of my visuals. Some of the county names are also found in other US states. I created calculated and conditional columns so that Power BI would recognize the county names found only in the state of Maryland.
