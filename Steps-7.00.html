1.      Click on the red button  above to download and save the Project Essentials folder on your local machine.
2.       Click on the raw file dataset link below and save the dataset on your device.
Raw Dataset Link: MDCOVID19 ConfirmedDeathsByCounty | Maryland's GIS Data Catalog
3.       Open Power BI Desktop, click on Get data on the Home ribbon. 
4.       Select Text/CSV from the drop-down list, then click Connect.
5.       Choose MD COVID19_Cumulative and New Deaths By County (2020 - 2023) from the Project Essentials folder and click Open. 
6.       Once the preview is displayed, click on Load.
7.       Rename it as MD_COVID19_DEATHS.
